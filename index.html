DinoSave Community Rules
These rules exist to keep DinoSave fair, safe and fun for every player. Breaking them may result in a warning, kick, or permanent ban.
01
🚫 No Cheating or Hacking
Using exploits, bots, scripts, modified clients, or any unauthorized tool to gain an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden. This includes manipulating your coins, DNA, dinos, or any other game data outside of normal gameplay.
⚠️ Penalty: Immediate permanent ban, no appeal
02
💸 No Selling Accounts
Buying, selling, trading, or transferring your DinoSave account to another person is not allowed. Accounts are personal and non-transferable. This includes trading accounts for real money, in-game items, or any other compensation.
⚠️ Penalty: Permanent ban of all accounts involved
03
👑 Admin Decisions Are Final
All decisions made by DinoSave admins regarding bans, warnings, events, or any in-game matter are final. Arguing, harassing, or attempting to circumvent admin decisions will result in further penalties. You may submit a respectful appeal once per ban.
⚠️ Penalty: Extended ban for harassing staff
By playing DinoSave you agree to follow these rules. Rules may be updated at any time — admins will announce major changes via Global Alert.

🦖⚖️🦕
The DinoSave Code
These rules exist to keep DinoSave a safe, fun and fair place for all players. Breaking them can result in a kick or permanent ban.
01
🤝
Respect Every Player
Treat all players with respect. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind. DinoSave is for everyone.
⚠️ Penalty: Immediate ban
02
🚫
No Cheating or Hacking
Do not exploit bugs, use external tools, scripts, or bots to gain unfair advantages. If you find a bug, report it to the admin instead.
⚠️ Penalty: Permanent ban + progress reset
03
💬
No Spamming
Do not flood the chat with repeated messages, symbols, or links. Keep conversations meaningful and on-topic.
⚠️ Penalty: Kick → Ban on repeat
04
🔒
No Account Sharing or Selling
Your account is yours only. Do not share, sell, or transfer your account to another person. One person per account.
⚠️ Penalty: Account suspension
05
🧒
Keep it Family Friendly
No inappropriate language, adult content, or offensive usernames. DinoSave is for all ages — keep it clean!
⚠️ Penalty: Username reset or ban
06
🤝
Fair Trading Only
All dino trades must be agreed upon by both players. Do not scam other players in trades. Attempted scams will be reviewed.
⚠️ Penalty: Trade ban → Account ban
07
👑
Admin Decisions are Final
The DinoSave admin team has the final say on all matters. Respect their decisions. You may appeal a ban by contacting support.
⚠️ Arguing with admins may extend penalties
08
🦖
Have Fun & Save Dinos!
Most importantly — enjoy the game! Help other players, explore the world, collect all 37 dinos, and be part of the DinoSave community.
✅ Reward: The best gaming experience!
